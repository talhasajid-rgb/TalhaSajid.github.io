About

Simulation-focused engineering profile

My professional work focuses on applying CFD, fluid dynamics, heat and mass transfer, numerical methods, mathematical modeling, simulation, and engineering data analysis to solve complex engineering problems. I work with both physical understanding and computational tools to study flow behavior, evaluate thermal and fluid systems, and convert simulation results into clear technical insight for design and decision-making.

In my recent projects, I have worked on supersonic reactive and non-reactive flow around a heated cylinder, Dynamic Mode Decomposition with Control, and analytical coolant-flow modeling for automobile radiator tubes. These experiences have strengthened my ability to build reliable models, interpret complex results, question assumptions carefully, and communicate engineering findings in a practical way for research, design, and multidisciplinary collaboration.
